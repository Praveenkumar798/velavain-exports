Our Story

Based in the coastal city of Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh, VELAVAIN Exports has established itself as a leading exporter of premium Indian spices and fresh seafood. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the authentic flavors of India and the freshest catch from our waters to tables around the world.

With years of experience in international trade, we understand the importance of quality, consistency, and reliability. Every product that leaves our facility carries our commitment to excellence.
